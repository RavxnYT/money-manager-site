## Delete your data without deleting your account

If you only want your financial data deleted (accounts, transactions, budgets, savings, loans, etc.) but keep your login account:

1. Either use the “Delete My Data” option inside the app ,
2. Or send an email to **joeboulos94@gmail.com** from the same email you used to sign up, with the subject: **Data deletion request – Money Management App**.

We will delete your financial data from our active systems, while keeping your login account so you can continue using the app.
