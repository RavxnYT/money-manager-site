# Account and Data Deletion – Money Management App

If you want to delete your account and all associated data from the Money Management App:

1. Send an email to **joeboulos94@gmail.com** from the same email address you used to sign up in the app.
2. In the subject line, write: **Account deletion request – Money Management App**.
3. In the body of the email, confirm that you want your account and all associated data deleted.

### What will be deleted

When we process your request, we will delete:

- Your user account (email and profile)
- Your financial data stored in the app:
  - Accounts / wallets
  - Transactions
  - Budgets
  - Savings goals and progress
  - Bills and recurring payments
  - Loans and loan payments
- Any other data directly linked to your account in our database.

### What may be retained

We may keep limited data for a short time if needed:

- Technical logs or backups that are kept for security, anti‑fraud, or legal compliance.  
These are automatically deleted after the applicable retention period.

If you have any questions, contact **joeboulos94@gmail.com**.
